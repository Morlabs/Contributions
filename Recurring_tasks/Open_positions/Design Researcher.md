## Role Overview:
As a Design Researcher within the Product Sub-DAO at Trinity DAO, you are tasked with uncovering insights about user behaviors, needs, and motivations to inform and inspire design decisions. Your work will directly contribute to the development of user-centered products, ensuring they are not only functional but also resonate deeply with our target audience.

## Responsibilities:

- **Conduct User Research:** Plan and execute various user research methods, including surveys, interviews, usability testing, and ethnographic studies, to gather deep insights into user needs and experiences.
- **Synthesize Findings:** Analyze and synthesize research data to identify key user insights, pain points, and opportunities for product improvement and innovation.
- **Collaborate with Design and Product Teams:** Work closely with UI/UX designers and product managers to translate research findings into actionable design strategies and product features.
- **Advocate for Users:** Serve as a voice of the user within the product team, ensuring that user insights drive the design process and product decisions.
- **Develop Research Strategies:** Design research strategies that address both immediate and long-term product goals, continuously seeking to understand and predict user needs and market trends.

## Qualifications:

- Proven experience in design research, UX research, or a similar role, with a portfolio demonstrating impact on product design and strategy.
- Strong understanding of qualitative and quantitative research methodologies and when to apply them.
- Excellent analytical skills, with the ability to translate complex research findings into clear, actionable insights.
- Strong communication and collaboration skills, capable of working effectively with cross-functional teams.
- A passion for user-centered design and an empathetic approach to understanding user needs and behaviors.

## Application Process:

#### Fork the Repository
To get started, **fork the Open Positions repository** to your GitHub account, creating a workspace for your application.

#### Create a New File for Your Application
Within your forked repository, **create a Markdown (.md) file** named `Product-Sub-DAO-DesignResearcher_Application_[YourGitHubUsername].md`. This organization helps streamline the application review process.

#### Fill Out Your Application
Follow the **template provided in the README** of the Open Positions repository to guide your application. Make sure to include details about your background, your interest in the Design Researcher role, qualifications, and examples of your past work or projects that demonstrate your research expertise.

#### Submit a Pull Request
Once your application is complete, **submit a pull request (PR)** to the original Open Positions repository. Ensure your PR is clearly titled, for example, "Application for Design Researcher by @YourGitHubUsername," to formally submit your application.

#### Review Process
Your application will be reviewed on GitHub, where we value **transparent feedback and discussions**. We're excited to see how your research skills can contribute to the development of intuitive and impactful designs within our DAO.
